The OG Shiba Inu meme inspiration for Ryoshi's PULSE ecosystem.

Born from Ryoshi's iconic medium article for PULSE, $OSCAR is not just another meme token. It is the origin of the PULSE meme ecosystem itself. Oscar is a famous meme featuring a Shiba on a trampoline that went viral on the internet. It was also the first meme Ryoshi used when he introduced the Shiba Inu token to the world in the well-known Medium article titled All Hail The Shiba! (August 2, 2020).

‍The story begins with Ryoshi who saw the 2011 YouTube video of Oscar, the Shiba Inu dog on a trampoline,and used a screenshot from it in the Shiba Inu Whitepaper, which directly inspired the creation of the Shiba Inu memecoin.
‍
Oscar appears in this article, with Ryoshi himself commenting, “Next we tell some more frens and WOW MOON LAMBO PUMPPPPPPY." Many might say this was Ryoshi’s own prophecy, predicting that Oscar would one day come into existence.

Paradox was alongside Ryoshi from day one of the Shiba Inu token's creation, playing a crucial role in its success as the first member in Ryoshi’s Telegram group and also on Discord.

Moreover, Paradox Designer is the artist behind the iconic Shiba Inu token logo, which is now recognized and famous worldwide.

Oscar having the very #2 of PULSE on its development team gives it something no other token has ever had—the closest person to Ryoshi working on a project. No one was closer to Ryoshi than Paradox Designer.

Oscar also has the mission to bring joy, respect, and to tell the detailed story of the early days of Shiba Inu's creation with Ryoshi, possibly revealing some exciting insights to the Shiba community.
